About Us

Located in Jamaica, Bawlplatt Studios is a fast growing music recording studios that offers a space for musicians and bands to practice their music before their shows or gigs. In addition, we provides the lastest equipment for the bands to mix their music, and preview their sessions.

Since our launch in December of 2002, we have made it our mission to improve the quality of music in Jamaica. Our founder (Liam Hands-Green) is verse in the musical arts himself, and has always had a passion for it. "Music is the soundtrack to life" is what he always says.

Since then, we have hosted over 20 successful musicians and bands. Bawlplatt has been slowly rising to the number one spot of 'Top Recording Studios' in Jamaica.

Currently, the studio can only host one band per session. This may change as we have hopes of expaning in the future. In addition, a session must be booked before hand, that is, it cannot be booked on the same day as the session, and the session cannot be booked for more than 4 hours.
